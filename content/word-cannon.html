Free Word Cannon typing game — no sign-up, no download, no catch. Words fire out of a cannon and arc slowly across the screen. Type a word to blast it out of the air before it lands. It is a fun, fast way to level up from typing single letters to typing whole words on sight.

The words start short and easy, then grow longer as your score climbs — but the pace stays friendly, so it is always doable. Miss three words and the round ends, then you reload and go again.

How to play

Press Start game and words begin launching from the cannon in the bottom-left. To take a word down, type its first letter — this locks your aim onto it — then type the rest of the letters to blast it. Finish a word and it pops for points equal to its length. If a word lands before you complete it, you lose a life; three lost lives ends the round. There is no mouse and no Enter key: just type.

What it trains

Reading a whole word and typing it in one smooth burst — rather than pecking one letter at a time — is the leap that separates slow typists from fast ones. Word Cannon rewards exactly that flow. Locking onto a word and finishing it under a gentle time pressure trains your eyes and fingers to work a word ahead, which is the same skill you use when typing an email or a chat message quickly and cleanly.

Tips for a higher score

Pick the word closest to landing and commit to finishing it before starting another. Keep your fingers on the home row so common letters are a short hop away. Accuracy beats mashing — a mistyped letter simply does nothing, so aim for clean keystrokes rather than frantic ones. As the words get longer, keep a steady rhythm instead of rushing the ending, which is where most typos sneak in.

Frequently asked questions

Is it really free? Yes. No sign-up, no email, no download, and no paywall — just open the page and play.

Do you store my scores or what I type? No. The game runs entirely in your browser, nothing is uploaded, and refreshing starts a fresh round. There is no account and no server keeping a record.

How do I aim at a specific word? Type its first letter. That locks your cannon onto the nearest matching word; then just type the rest of it.

Does it work on a phone? It is designed for a physical keyboard. You can play on a phone with the on-screen keyboard, but it is slower and best treated as casual fun.
