Nifty Utilities has no backend server, database, user accounts, or endpoint capable of receiving your tool inputs. Files and entries are processed inside your browser. We cannot view, capture, or store them.

How to play

Press Start game and type the word shown in the box. As soon as it matches, it clears itself and the next word appears — there is no Enter to press and no button to click. Keep the words flowing for a full minute, then check your total and your WPM.

What it trains

Reading a whole word and typing it in one clean burst — rather than pecking letter by letter — is the habit that separates slow typists from fast ones. Word Sprint drills that flow under a friendly time pressure, which is exactly the rhythm you use when writing a message or an email quickly. The gentle ramp to longer words stretches your range without ever feeling unfair.

Tips for a higher score

Keep your fingers on the home row and let each finger cover its own keys. Read the whole word before you start typing it, so your fingers can flow instead of stutter. Accuracy matters: a typo means the word will not clear until you fix it, so smooth and correct beats fast and messy. And relax your hands — tension slows you down more than anything.

Frequently asked questions

Is it really free? Yes. No sign-up, no email, no download, and no paywall.

Do you store my scores or what I type? No. Everything runs in your browser and nothing is uploaded. There is no account and no server keeping a record.

How is the WPM calculated? It uses the standard five-characters-per-word rule over the time elapsed, so it lines up with our typing speed test. For a careful measurement, take the full test.

Does it work on a phone? You can play on a phone with the on-screen keyboard, but it is designed for a physical keyboard where real speed is built.

Important

Word Sprint is a free typing game for practice and fun. It is a learning aid, not a formal typing certification, and nothing is saved or sent anywhere.
