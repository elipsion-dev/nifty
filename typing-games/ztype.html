How to play

Just start typing. Type the first letter of any incoming word to lock your aim onto it, then type the rest of the word to blast that ship out of the sky. Clear each wave before the ships reach you. There is nothing to install and no mouse required — it is pure keyboard.

What it trains

ZType is a superb workout for typing whole words quickly and accurately while your attention is split across a moving screen — the same skill you use when typing fast in the real world. Because you choose which word to attack, it also trains quick word recognition and target-switching, which sharpens both speed and focus.
